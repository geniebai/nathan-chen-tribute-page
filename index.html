A timeline of Nathan's skating career

  • 1999 - The legend is born in Salt Lake City, Utah.
  • 2002 - He begins figure skating.
  • 2003 - He enters his first skating competition.
  • 2010 - Becomes the youngest novice champion at the 2010 US Championships.
  • 2012 - Wins the junior men's title at the 2012 US Championships.
  • 2012 - Makes international skating debut, and wins the novice men's title at the 2012 Gardena Spring Trophy.
  • 2012 - Wins gold at JGP Austria with the highest score of 222.00 achieved at a JGP event.
  • 2014 - At the 2014 US Championships, achieves the highest score from a combination spin of 4.86 among active male skaters.
  • 2016 - At the 2016 US Championships, becomes the first man to land four quad jumps in a long program.
  • 2017 - Wins the 2017 US Championships with the highest score of 318.47 in US figure skating history.
  • 2017 - At the 2017 CS US International Skating Classic, becomes the first man to land five different quads in competition.
  • 2018 - At the 2018 PyeongChang Winter Olympics, becomes the first man to attempt and land six quads in the free skate. He also earns the highest score of 215.08 in the free skate in Olympic history.
